About Us

At CakeHistory, we believe every cake tells a story — of joy, love, and celebration. Our mission is to deliver handcrafted cakes that bring smiles to every occasion.

🎯 Vision

To be a trusted and beloved brand for traditional sponge cakes, blending timeless baking traditions (古早味) with modern tastes to share authentic joy across generations.

💗 Mission

At Cake History, our mission is to craft soft, fresh, and affordable cakes that bring smiles to every occasion. We combine nostalgic recipes with creative presentation, connecting people through the warmth and sweetness of every slice.
